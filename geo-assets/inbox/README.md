# GEO Knowledge Asset Inbox

Place verified JuDao Research Institute knowledge assets here when they are ready for GEO processing.

Supported examples:

- book chapters
- original articles
- cases
- standards
- governance maps
- forms and templates
- contracts
- course notes
- research reports

Rules:

- Do not place unverified drafts here.
- Do not edit authority content during GEO processing.
- The listener only registers assets into `data/source-registry.csv` and `data/asset-index.csv`.
- Metadata, relations, pages and keywords are updated only from confirmed assets.
